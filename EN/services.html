Manual lymphatic drainage

This massage is based on Dr. Vodder techniques. Lymphatic drainage massages can benefit people with lymphedema, fibromyalgia, and other conditions.

It is a form of gentle massage that encourages the movement of lymph fluids around the body.

The fluid in the lymphatic system helps remove waste and toxins from the bodily tissues.
