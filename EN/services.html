Therapies practised

Deep tissue

Deep tissue massage is suitable for all people with problems with mustles of the whole body.

One of the most common reasons for people to have a deep tissue massage is the general aches and pains that fall under the broad definition of rheumatic pains, aching joints and muscle spasms.

The main underlying cause of many symptoms manifested in the body is stress.

Therapeutic massage

Therapeutic massage can help to relieve stress, insomnia, tense muscles and back pain, and is believed by many to improve circulation and digestion, tone the skin and help eliminate toxins.

Music may also be used as an aid to achieving a relaxing treatment.

It is recommended for your body, mind and spirit.

India head massage

It is a great stress buster, and although we call it a head massage, we incorporate the back, shoulders and arms.

This treatment is good for corporate clients as it is non-invasive and you do not need to remove any clothing.

The physical benefits that can be gained: Ease tight muscles, releases shoulders, therefore improving posture.

Manual lymphatic drainage

This massage is based on Dr. Vodder techniques. Lymphatic drainage massages can benefit people with lymphedema, fibromyalgia, and other conditions.

It is a form of gentle massage that encourages the movement of lymph fluids around the body.

The fluid in the lymphatic system helps remove waste and toxins from the bodily tissues.

Prenatal and childbirth massage

I provide support to women during pregnancy and during childbirth.

I can not only treat pregnant women with a relaxing massage concentrating on back, belly and feet aches, but I can also teach your partner to provide you with a massage.

The most comfortable position for expectant mothers is sideline positioning with supportive, soft cushions used to alleviate the extra strain on the lower back and pelvic areas when the pressure of massage techniques are applied.
